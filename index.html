Original Compositions

Skyline

“Skyline” is a bright and cinematic composition capturing the energy of a new day. The piece layers sweeping melodies with rhythmic undertones that resemble the movement and pulse of a city awakening.

Adrenaline

“Adrenaline” embodies intensity, speed, and focus — an instrumental track written to express the drive and persistence that defines progress. A blend of cinematic percussion and brass themes create its dynamic flow.

Arrangements

Shallow - A Star is Born (Arr. Luis Tovar)

A wind ensemble arrangement of Lady Gaga and Bradley Cooper’s “Shallow.” Reimagined for concert band with expressive brass voicing, dynamic swells, and rich harmonic textures that highlight the song’s emotional depth.

Interstellar Main Theme (Arr. Luis Tovar)

A concert band arrangement of Hans Zimmer’s iconic score. This rendition emphasizes the gradual build of intensity, focusing on tonal layering and harmonic momentum reflective of the film’s vastness.
