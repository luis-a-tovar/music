About Me

Hello! My name is Luis Tovar, and I am a senior at Virginia Tech pursuing a degree in Mechanical Engineering with a concentration in Dynamic Systems and Controls. I am deeply passionate about the intersection of engineering and music, constantly exploring how creativity and technical precision can coexist.

Through my experiences, I’ve developed a strong interest in design, acoustics, and system dynamics — all areas that bridge my analytical mindset and creative problem-solving approach. When I’m not working on engineering projects, you’ll often find me composing music, arranging pieces, or performing with different ensembles.
